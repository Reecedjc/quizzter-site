Support for Quizzter

If you need help with Quizzter, account access, custom quizzes, privacy questions, or deletion requests, contact us here.

Email Support Account Deletion Help

Account and sign-in help

Quizzter supports email and password sign-in, Google sign-in, and Apple sign-in on supported platforms. If you cannot access your account, include your sign-in method, device type, and the exact error message when contacting support.

Custom quiz and market help

If a quiz does not publish, import, or rate correctly, include the quiz title, what you were trying to do, and any message shown in the app.

Ads and reminders

Ads appear only on supported mobile platforms. Daily reminders are scheduled locally on the device. If reminders stop appearing, check your notification permissions and device battery settings.

Privacy requests

For privacy questions, account data concerns, or deletion follow-up, contact support and clearly mention that your request is about privacy or account deletion.

Contact

Email hello@quizzter.com and include as much detail as you can so the issue can be resolved faster.

What to include in a support request

Device details

Platform, device model, and app version if you know it.

What happened

The exact steps that caused the bug or the action you were trying to take.

What you saw

Any on-screen error message, screenshot, or sign-in method involved.
