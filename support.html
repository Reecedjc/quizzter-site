Account and sign-in help

Quizzter supports email and password sign-in, Google sign-in, and Apple sign-in on supported platforms. If you cannot access your account, include your sign-in method, device type, and the exact error message when contacting support.

Custom quiz and market help

If a quiz does not publish, import, or rate correctly, send the quiz title, what action you attempted, and any visible message from the app so the issue can be reproduced quickly.

Ads and reminders

Ads appear only on supported mobile platforms. Daily reminders are scheduled locally on the device. If reminders stop appearing, verify your notification permissions and device battery settings.

Privacy requests

For privacy questions, account data concerns, or deletion follow-up, email support and clearly mention that your request is about privacy or account deletion.

What to include in a support request

Device details

Platform, device model, and app version if you know it.

What happened

The exact steps that caused the bug or the action you were trying to take.

What you saw

Any on-screen error message, screenshot, or sign-in method involved.
