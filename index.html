What Quizzter covers

The app is built for broad recall practice rather than a single course. You can keep the built-in library, rearrange it, reset it, or layer in your own custom quiz sets.

Built-in recall modes

Practice Morse code, binary, capitals, countries, flags, periodic symbols, and memory flash prompts in one place.

Custom quiz creation

Create your own question decks, keep them private, or publish them for the community market when you want to share.

Short-session design

The app is designed for quick rounds, answer streaks, lightweight feedback, and enough repetition to keep the habit alive.

How accounts work

Accounts are optional. Local use works without sign-in, while cloud features unlock quiz sync, public usernames, market publishing, ratings, and saved session history.

Without an account

The app can store settings, streaks, display name, profile image path, and guest quiz content directly on the device.

With an account

Signed-in users can authenticate with email and password, Google, or Apple, claim a public username, save quiz data in the cloud, and publish decks to the community market.
