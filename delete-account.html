Google Play and user deletion guidance

Delete your Quizzter account

Quizzter allows signed-in users to delete their account directly inside the app. This page explains how the process works and what happens after deletion.

Delete your account in the app

1
Open Quizzter

Launch the app on the device where you normally use your account.

2
Go to Profile

Open the Profile screen from the app home area.

3
Choose Delete account

Confirm the deletion prompt when Quizzter asks if you want to permanently delete the account.

4
Sign in again if asked

Firebase may require a recent sign-in for security. If that happens, sign in again and repeat the deletion flow.

What is deleted

Deleting your account removes the cloud account and related cloud records tied to that account. This includes your Firebase Authentication account, your claimed username records, your private cloud-stored quizzes, your saved cloud session history, and quizzes you published to the community market along with their associated published storage payloads.

What may remain on your device

Some local-only data may remain on the device after account deletion, such as app preferences, streak information, or other locally stored settings. Removing the app or clearing local app storage may be required if you also want to erase local-only data from the device.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app to delete your account, contact the support address listed on the Support page and include the sign-in method you used for Quizzter.

Account deletion can be completed directly from the Quizzter app.
